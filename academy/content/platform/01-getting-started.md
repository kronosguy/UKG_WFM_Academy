# Getting Started with KronosGuy

## Welcome to the Platform

KronosGuy is a comprehensive Kronos/UKG Workforce Management (WFM) training and enablement platform. This guide will walk you through the foundational concepts and get you set up for success.

## What is KronosGuy?

KronosGuy provides:

- **Comprehensive Training**: Structured learning paths for all UKG WFM modules
- **Production-Grade Labs**: Real-world scenario simulations and hands-on practice
- **Knowledge Repository**: Complete reference documentation and data dictionaries
- **Community Resources**: Best practices, troubleshooting guides, and expert tips

## Core Concepts

### 1. Learning Paths

A learning path is a curated sequence of modules designed to teach you a specific aspect of UKG WFM:

- **Linear Paths**: Follow prerequisites in order (Foundation → Advanced)
- **Modular Paths**: Pick and choose based on your role
- **Certification Paths**: Complete structured programs with assessments

### 2. Three Learning Domains

#### Platform Knowledge (This Section)
Learn how KronosGuy works, including navigation, features, and account management.

#### Academy Learning (See: Academy Documentation)
Master UKG WFM systems through structured courses, labs, and assessments.

#### Knowledge Base (See: UKG Knowledge Base)
Access reference materials, APIs, troubleshooting, and data dictionaries.

### 3. Role-Based Learning

Your learning experience adapts to your role:

- **Administrator**: System configuration, user management, security
- **Manager**: Scheduling, labor analytics, team management
- **Operator**: Daily operations, time and attendance, scheduling
- **Analyst**: Reporting, forecasting, data analysis

## Your First Steps

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Navigate to the Account section
2. Click "Create Account" or "Sign In"
3. Complete your profile with your role and department
4. Select your primary learning focus area

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile

Update your profile to personalize your learning:

- **Role**: Your position (Administrator, Manager, Operator, etc.)
- **Department**: Your team or business unit
- **Experience Level**: Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced
- **Learning Goals**: What do you want to accomplish?

### Step 3: Select a Learning Path

Choose a structured learning path that matches your goals:

```
Recommended for Administrators:
  └─ UKG WFM Administration Fundamentals (8 hours)
     ├─ Module 1: System Architecture
     ├─ Module 2: User Management
     ├─ Module 3: Security & Permissions
     └─ Module 4: Configuration Basics

Recommended for Managers:
  └─ Workforce Scheduling Essentials (6 hours)
     ├─ Module 1: Scheduling Concepts
     ├─ Module 2: Creating Schedules
     ├─ Module 3: Labor Analytics
     └─ Module 4: Team Management

Recommended for Operators:
  └─ Time & Attendance Operations (4 hours)
     ├─ Module 1: Clocking In/Out
     ├─ Module 2: Timesheet Review
     ├─ Module 3: Absence Management
     └─ Module 4: Daily Operations
```

## Platform Navigation

### Top Navigation Bar

- **Home**: Return to the main dashboard
- **Academy**: Browse and access all courses
- **Knowledge Base**: Search reference materials
- **My Learning**: Track your progress
- **Settings**: Customize your experience

### Left Sidebar

The sidebar provides context-sensitive navigation:

- **Current Module**: Shows your progress in the active course
- **Related Topics**: Suggested related content
- **Your Notes**: Quick access to bookmarks and notes
- **Quick Links**: Role-based shortcuts

## Understanding Your Dashboard

Your personal dashboard shows:

1. **Progress Overview**: Your learning statistics
2. **Current Modules**: In-progress courses
3. **Recommended Next**: AI-suggested next steps
4. **Achievements**: Badges and certifications earned
5. **Time Tracking**: Hours invested in learning

## Common Questions

### How do I track my progress?

Visit **My Learning** to see:
- Courses completed (%)
- Modules finished
- Total learning hours
- Badges and certifications

### Can I download course materials?

Yes! Each module includes downloadable:
- PDF guides
- Checklists
- Configuration templates
- Reference materials

### How do I get help?

Multiple support options available:

- **Knowledge Base Search**: Find answers instantly
- **Help Chat**: Connect with support agents (Mon-Fri, 9am-6pm EST)
- **Community Forum**: Ask questions, see solutions
- **Video Tutorials**: Visual step-by-step guides

### What if I need to reset my progress?

You can reset progress on individual modules. Visit Settings > Learning > Reset Progress. Your achievements remain, but module progress resets to 0%.
